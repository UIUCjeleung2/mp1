Fun Facts

Polar bears (Ursus maritimus) live in the Arctic Circle, including places such as Northern Canada, Greenland, and Alaska.

They can run fast, up to 25mph. However, they can get hot very quickly, so they usually walk.

Being one of the best swimmers among land mammals, they can swim up to 60 miles without stopping in search of seals.
